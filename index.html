What Is a Car Stereo?

A car stereo is the sound system that's installed in a car. It includes a head unit, which is the part that you see in the dashboard, and it usually has a display that shows what's playing.

The head unit is connected to speakers, which are mounted in the car's interior. There is also a power amplifier, which boosts the signal from the head unit so that the speakers can produce louder sound.

And finally, there's the subwoofer. This is a special speaker that's designed to produce low-frequency sounds, and it's usually placed in the trunk of the car.
